The gentle hum of a quiet morning is perhaps one of the most underrated experiences in modern life. 
Before the city fully wakes, there is a brief window where the air feels cleaner and the world moves at a slower, more intentional pace. 
Sunlight, filtering through morning haze, turns simple scenes into quiet masterpieces, casting long, dramatic shadows that make ordinary trees and rooftops look like art. 
In these moments, sipping coffee or watching the birds begin their day brings a sense of calm that is difficult to find once the frantic energy of the afternoon takes over. 	It is a daily reminder that peace is often found not in grand events, but in the soft, uncomplicated moments in between, waiting to be noticed.
